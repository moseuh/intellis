Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I use the service charge analysis feature?

Service charge analysis is typically needed twice a year when you receive your service charge statements. Our Standard and Premium plans include this bi-annual analysis, while Basic plan users can purchase this service as needed.

What is the difference between basic and advanced service charge analysis?

Basic analysis provides a simple review of charges and identifies obvious discrepancies. Advanced analysis includes historical trend comparison, detailed line-item verification, and expert recommendations for potential challenges to unreasonable charges.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a limit to the size of documents I can upload?

Basic and Standard plans have a 20MB per document limit. Premium plans allow documents up to 50MB. If you have larger documents, please contact our support team for assistance.
