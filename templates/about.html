Our Founder's Journey

The story behind IntelliLease is deeply personal, rooted in real experiences navigating the UK leasehold system.

1997: First Leasehold Purchase

Like many Londoners, I purchased my first leasehold flat with excitement, only to discover the complexities and hidden costs that came with it. Service charge increases of 15% year-on-year with little explanation left me frustrated and searching for answers.

2007: Second Leasehold Purchase

My second leasehold purchase brought new challenges. When the building faced significant structural repairs, leaseholders were presented with an unexpected £12,000 bill each. The lack of prior communication and transparency in how these costs were calculated created tension and distrust between residents and management.

2018: Joining the RTM Board

Determined to improve the situation, I joined our building's Right to Manage board. Over the past five years, I gained insider knowledge of property management challenges, including the disconnect between stakeholders and the inefficiencies in traditional management systems.

2025: The Birth of IntelliLease

Drawing on my experiences as both a leaseholder and RTM board member, I founded IntelliLease with a clear mission: to create technology that brings transparency, efficiency, and trust to the leasehold sector. What started as a personal frustration has evolved into a solution that benefits all stakeholders.

"After experiencing the frustrations of leasehold ownership firsthand, I became convinced that technology could bridge the information gap and create a more transparent system for everyone involved. IntelliLease isn't just a business—it's a solution to a problem I've lived with for decades."
