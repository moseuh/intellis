Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IntelliLease and leasehold management.

IntelliLease can analyze all standard UK leasehold documents, including original leases, lease extension proposals, lease variations, and related correspondence. Our system is trained on thousands of UK leasehold documents to ensure accurate analysis.

We take security extremely seriously. All documents are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use bank-level security protocols, and your documents are only accessible to you. After analysis, you can choose to delete all uploaded files from our servers.

No legal knowledge is required. IntelliLease is designed to translate complex legal language into clear, understandable terms. Our system highlights important clauses and explains their implications in plain English, making leasehold management accessible to everyone.

IntelliLease is designed to complement legal advice, not replace it. Our tools help you better understand your leasehold documents and identify potential issues, which can make your interactions with solicitors more efficient and potentially reduce legal costs. For complex legal matters, we always recommend consulting with a qualified solicitor.
