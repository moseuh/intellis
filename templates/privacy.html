Privacy Policy for IntelliLease

At IntelliLease ("we", "our", "us"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our platform, services, and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Property address and leasehold details
  • Uploaded documents (leases, service charge bills, ground rent statements, etc.)
  • Payment information (processed via third-party processors such as Stripe)
  • Technical data (IP address, device info, usage data)

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our lease audit and RTM management services
  • Analyze and compare lease and billing data
  • Send notifications and service-related communications
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent (where required)
  • Contractual necessity (to provide the services you sign up for)
  • Legitimate interest (to improve our platform)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., cloud storage, analytics tools, payment processors)
  • Legal authorities, if required by law
  • Third-party APIs used to process or analyze your uploaded documents (e.g., OpenAI, OCR services)

All data processors comply with UK GDPR through Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).

Data Storage & Security

We store data on secure cloud servers within the UK/EU. We apply encryption, access controls, and routine audits to protect your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: Info@intellilease.co

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Cookies

Our platform may use cookies for functionality and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices. Material updates will be notified to users by email or platform notice.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, contact our Data Protection Officer:

Email: info@intellilease.co

This Privacy Policy is compliant with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.
